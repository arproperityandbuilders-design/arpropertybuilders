Sheet A—01 / About the Firm

We Treat Every Drawing Like It Has to Outlive Us

Founded in 2002, AR Property and Builders began with a simple standard: no shortcuts between the drawing board and the finished structure. Today, our in-house architects, structural engineers, and site teams deliver residential and commercial developments across Gulshan, Banani, Bashundhara, Uttara, and beyond — every one of them RAJUK-approved and built to hold up long after handover.
